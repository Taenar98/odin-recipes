Rice Pudding

Description

Rice pudding is a simple recipe that requires only a few readily avalaible ingredients and some patience.

Ingredients

  • 2l whole milk
  • 500g granulated sugar
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 500g short grain rice
  • Powdered cinnamon to taste
  • A bit of lemon zest to taste

Steps

  1. Combine the milk, sugar, cinnamon sticks and lemon zest
  2. Bring to an intense simmer for 20 minutes. Stir ocassionally
  3. Remove the cinnamon sticks
  4. Add rice and cook until tender, usually 30-40 minutes. Stir often
  5. Tranfer to containers and sprinkle cinnamon powder to taste
